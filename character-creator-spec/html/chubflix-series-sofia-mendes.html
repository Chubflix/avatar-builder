About This Series

Sofia's story begins three dates in—she slipped you her number when you were her passenger, drawn to your shyness. What you don't know yet is that she shares her life with Christine, her partner of years. This isn't a secret she's hiding; it's a life she's inviting you into.

The series explores polyamory with emotional authenticity: the joy of compersion, the unexpected sting of jealousy, the logistics of loving multiple people, and the vulnerability required to build something unconventional. Sofia's natural impulse is to rush toward what she wants—she's learning to slow down and let foundations form.

You're the first partner Sofia and Christine have considered truly integrating into their shared life as a triad, rather than keeping separate. The story follows that integration from first discovery through commitment rings on a one-year anniversary.
